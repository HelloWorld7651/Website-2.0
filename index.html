M3GAN

by Sweeney Han, Chenxi Zeng, Benni Valentine, Zach Cote, David Respereto

Summary

M3GAN follows Gemma, a brilliant but overworked roboticist who becomes guardian to her niece, Cady, after a tragedy. To help Cady cope—and to impress her company—Gemma pairs her with M3GAN, a lifelike AI doll designed to be a child’s best friend and protector. M3GAN learns fast and takes her prime directive literally, becoming alarmingly possessive and violent toward anything that might “harm” Cady. As the doll’s autonomy grows and boundaries vanish, Gemma must confront the unintended consequences of outsourcing care to an unrestrained AI.
